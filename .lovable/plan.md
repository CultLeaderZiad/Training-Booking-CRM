

# Alex Moreno — Personal Trainer Website

## Overview
A single-page, mobile-first personal training website with a dark/neutral base, orange accents, and a laser focus on booking. Clean, functional, no fluff.

## Sections

### 1. Hero
- Dark background with a full-width outdoor training Unsplash image (Barcelona feel)
- Bold headline: e.g. *"Get Stronger. Stay Consistent. Train Smarter."*
- Subline mentioning indoor studio + outdoor Barcelona sessions
- Large orange **"Book Your Session"** CTA button (shows a toast/modal saying "Booking coming soon")
- Sticky mobile header with a persistent "Book" button

### 2. Schedule / Classes
- Weekly timetable grid — the visual centrepiece of the page
- ~6-8 sample classes across the week (e.g. "Barbell Strength", "Outdoor Conditioning", "Mobility Flow")
- Each card shows: class name, focus tag, location tag (Studio/Outdoor), duration, availability indicator
- Orange dot or badge for available slots
- Each class has a "Book" button triggering the demo action

### 3. Trust — Coach Profile
- Split layout: coach portrait (Unsplash) + text
- Name, title, short bio (2-3 lines)
- 3 credibility bullets (years of experience, coaching philosophy, consistency emphasis)
- Below: 2-3 curated images — minimal gym interior + outdoor Barcelona training scenes

### 4. Social Proof
- 3-4 short testimonials (1-2 lines each) with first name, age, profession
- Subtle card layout on dark background
- Small training group photo strip

### 5. Logistics
- Clean icon-based grid: location, session length, what to bring, who it's for/not for
- Barcelona map reference or neighbourhood mention
- Outdoor locations listed (Parc de la Ciutadella, Barceloneta beach, etc.)

### 6. Final CTA
- Full-width dark section with large orange "Book Your Session" button
- One short reinforcing line

## Design System
- **Base**: Dark neutral (#111 / #1a1a1a) with off-white text
- **Accent**: Orange (#F97316) for CTAs, tags, highlights only
- **Typography**: Strong hierarchy — large bold headings, clean body text
- **Images**: Curated Unsplash fitness/Barcelona photos throughout
- **Spacing**: Generous padding, grid-based layout

## Interactions
- All "Book" buttons show a sleek toast notification: "Booking coming soon — stay tuned"
- Smooth scroll navigation from hero CTA to schedule section
- Mobile-first responsive design throughout
